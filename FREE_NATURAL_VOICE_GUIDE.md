# FREE Natural Voice Guide - Get the Best Voice Quality

## The Best FREE Option: Microsoft Edge Browser

Microsoft Edge has the BEST free TTS voices built-in. They sound much more natural than Chrome or Firefox.

### Step 1: Use Microsoft Edge
1. Download Microsoft Edge: https://www.microsoft.com/edge (FREE)
2. Open your school bell system in Edge

### Step 2: Check Available Voices
1. Open your school system in Edge
2. Press **F12** to open Developer Tools
3. Click "Console" tab
4. Paste this command and press Enter:

```javascript
speechSynthesis.getVoices().forEach(v => console.log(v.name + ' - ' + v.lang))
```

### Step 3: Look for These GOOD Voices
Edge includes these natural-sounding voices:
- **Microsoft Aria Online (Natural)** - Female, very clear
- **Microsoft Jenny Online (Natural)** - Female, friendly
- **Microsoft Guy Online (Natural)** - Male, professional
- **Microsoft Davis Online (Natural)** - Male, casual
- **Microsoft Zira** - Female, clear (older but good)
- **Microsoft David** - Male, clear (older but good)

### Step 4: Select the Voice in Settings
1. Go to **Settings** in your app
2. Under "Default Voice", select:
   - **Azure Aria** (uses Edge's Aria voice)
   - **Azure Jenny** (uses Edge's Jenny voice)
   - **Azure Guy** (uses Edge's Guy voice)
   - **Azure Davis** (uses Edge's Davis voice)
3. Click **Save Settings**

### Step 5: Test It
1. Go to Timetable
2. Create a test announcement for 1 minute from now
3. Wait and listen - it should sound MUCH better in Edge!

## Why Edge is Better

**Chrome/Firefox**: Basic robotic voices
**Microsoft Edge**: Natural-sounding voices (same technology as Azure)

Edge uses Microsoft's neural TTS technology for FREE!

## Alternative: Install Better Voices on Windows

If you're on Windows 10/11, you can install additional voices:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Time & Language** → **Speech**
2. Click "Add voices"
3. Download voices like:
   - **Microsoft Aria** (English US)
   - **Microsoft Jenny** (English US)
   - **Microsoft Guy** (English US)

These will work in Edge and make the voice even better!
