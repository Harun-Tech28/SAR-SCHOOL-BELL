GHANA SCHOOL BELL SYSTEM - QUICK START GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IMPORTANT: THE APP RUNS IN THE BACKGROUND!
When you double-click the app, it DOES start - but it runs in the
system tray (background) so bells can ring even when closed.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
HOW TO OPEN THE APP WINDOW:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Look at the BOTTOM-RIGHT corner of your screen
        (where the clock and date are)

STEP 2: Click the UP ARROW (^) icon
        This shows "hidden icons"

STEP 3: Find the BELL ICON or "Ghana School Bell" icon

STEP 4: CLICK the icon to open the window
        OR
        RIGHT-CLICK and select "Show App"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
FIRST TIME SETUP:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open the app window (see steps above)

2. Click "TIMETABLE" on the left side

3. Click "ADD TIMETABLE" button

4. Enter your school's bell times:
   - Bell name (e.g., "Morning Assembly")
   - Time (e.g., 8:00 AM)
   - Days (Monday-Friday)
   - Bell sound

5. Click "SAVE"

6. Repeat for all your school bells

7. DONE! Bells will ring automatically

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CHECKING IF APP IS RUNNING:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Press Ctrl+Shift+Esc to open Task Manager
Look for "Ghana School Bell System.exe"
If you see it, the app IS running!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
IMPORTANT NOTES:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✓ The app MUST be running for bells to ring
✓ You can close the window - app keeps running in background
✓ Your timetables are saved automatically
✓ To FULLY QUIT: Right-click tray icon → "Quit App"
✓ Keep all files in the same folder together

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TROUBLESHOOTING:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: I double-clicked but nothing happened
Solution: The app IS running! Look in system tray (see steps above)

Problem: I can't find the icon in system tray
Solution: 1. Click the UP ARROW (^) to show hidden icons
          2. Check Task Manager - if app is there, it's working

Problem: Bells are not ringing
Solution: 1. Make sure app is running (check Task Manager)
          2. Check your timetable is saved
          3. Check computer volume is not muted

Problem: I want to close the app completely
Solution: Right-click tray icon → "Quit App"
