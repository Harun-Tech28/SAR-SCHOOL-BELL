## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### No Sound When Testing?

1. **Check Volume**
   - Ensure system volume is up
   - Check browser isn't muted

2. **Check Browser**
   - Use Chrome/Edge (best support)
   - Allow audio permissions

3. **Try Different Bell**
   - Some bells are quieter than others
   - Brass School Bell is loudest

### Voice Not Playing?

1. **Wait for Bell to Finish**
   - Bell plays first, then voice
   - Don't click multiple times

2. **Check Internet**
   - Voice synthesis needs internet
   - Bell sounds work offline

3. **Try Different Voice**
   - Some voices may not be available
   - OpenAI voices most reliable
